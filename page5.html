Frequently asked questions

Check out our compilation of frequently asked questions with our responses to them.

Check out our catalogue

  • How do I schedule an appointment?
    To schedule an appointment, kindly reach out to us via channels provided on our contact page or sign up to book an appointment on your dashboard.
  • Does Psychelapcare accept insurance?
    We currently do not accept insurance but payment can be made via bank transfer or through your dashboard. In case of transfer, you are to send your evidence of payment to the email Psychelapcare@gmail.com or call this number 08069644758  for confirmation.
  • What clinic location should I choose?
    We primarily provide telehealth services to everyone across Nigeria and outside for better experience at your comfort zone. Our Services are delivered via google meet, zoom meeting and WhatsApp platform but our clinicians can provide inperson and in the community based on special request and this will attract extra charges. Contact us for more information.
  • How far out is scheduling?
  • Our services run on West African Time zone, through 24hrs/day Monday - Friday, between 10am - 4on Saturday and we are unavailable on Sundays.
  • Who is the best counselor for me to see?
    Our Intake Coordinators will work with you to match you to a clinician to serve your needs. For more information, call 08069644758 or email Psychelapcare@gmail.com
  • How many sessions will I need to feel better?
    Usually a minimum of 6 sessions is recommended for most therapeutic services, however you may need more depending on the severity of your symptoms. You can decide to pay for a session to see how it goes but we always advice you sign up for 3 sessions with us for a start for better experience as the first session is usually more of psychological evaluations, exploring possible areas of experiences and history taking to develop proper treatment plans for you.
  • What is your rescheduling, cancellation and refunding policy for appointments?
  • If you need to cancel your appointment, please call us at (phone number). Cancellation and rescheduling of appointment should be done within 24hrs , failure to adhere may attract extra charges. Refunding is only considered if a therapist didn’t show up at the time of your appointment.
