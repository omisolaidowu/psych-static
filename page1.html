How we came about

Here's a bit of our story of how we came to be.

The name Psychelapcare come about, as a result of our vision to provide proper psychological evaluations through our clinical observation and psychological assessment instruments in order to make diagnosis and provide adequate care needed in that area, through therapy or medication where and when necessary to our users

Our mission

The mission of Psychelapcare is to work together as a team to bring hope, care and recovery to the people we serve and also follow them up.

Our vision

Our vision and dream is to see you smile again and take control of your life through our therapeutic and training services. As much as we use different theoretical approaches to help you heal better, we like to dwell on the humanistic school of thought that believe in self-actualization and human happiness which brings us to our goal.

Our goal

In Psychelapcare, one important goal is to bring back happiness and hope to our clients with our slogan that says ‘We are happy when you are better’.

About

We are mental health team, working together to make sure our client enjoys right services from the best platform. Our team member includes; Consultant psychiatrist with over 10 years of experience, Ph.D. licensed clinical psychologist with over 5 years of clinical experience, neuro-developmental psychologist with many years of experience and many others.
